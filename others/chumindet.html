--- 2. How to Make a Model of Plant and Animal Cell (Light Microscope View)*
*Plant Cell:*
- Use a rectangular base for the cell wall.
- Include a large central vacuole, chloroplasts, nucleus, cytoplasm, and mitochondria.
- Use green clay for chloroplasts and blue for vacuole.
*Animal Cell:*
- Use a round/oval base for cell membrane.
- Include nucleus, mitochondria, cytoplasm, and smaller vacuoles.
- No chloroplasts or cell wall.
3. Short Notes: How to Make Models of Plant & Animal Cells (Light Microscope)*
- *Materials Needed:* Clay, cardboard, glue, markers.
- *Plant Cell:* Add cell wall, chloroplasts, and large vacuole.
- *Animal Cell:* Round shape, no cell wall or chloroplasts.
- *Label Organelles:* Nucleus, cytoplasm, mitochondria, etc.
- *Use color coding* for easy identification.
